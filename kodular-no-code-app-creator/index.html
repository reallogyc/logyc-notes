Kodular - No Code App Creator 

Maayur December 24, 2020 Articles
Interested in Making Android App or have a good idea, but don't know to code. Don't worry we got you a new toy !!
 

Starting here is so simple, just head to their homepage, click on Create Apps and log in using any desired option. It will take you to Kodular Creator. Where you will turn Ideas to Apps. There click on create new project, a pop-up will appear enter your project name then configure your project setting and click on Finish and Congratulations your Project is Created 🥳

Now as your project is created, let's see how to use the Creator. When your Project is Opened, you will see an Interface for creating apps called Designer. Where it has Component Platte at Left an Android Device Mockup at Center and a Properties tab at Right along with some Buttons in the top menu.

‌I personally recommend and use Kodular for Creating Professional Applications. I am also Available on Kodular Community and had Published Several Basic/Advance Guides for Kodular. You can also ask your Issue/Query there. The Kodular Community is One of the best and Active Community in all AI2 Distros. The Users and Staff there are so helpful and give their best to give you the solution to your problem.
