Kodular - A Modern App Creator, is a Distribution of MIT App Inventor. A Blocky based Android App Development Platform. Developing Applications is as easy as Drag-Drop-Block-Repeat.

Kodular comes with a very clean and simple Material UI giving the Creator the best looks and UX.

Starting here is so simple, just head to their homepage, click on Create Apps and log in using any desired option. It will take you to Kodular Creator. Where you will turn Ideas to Apps. Now as your project is created, let's see how to use the Creator. When your Project is Opened, you will see an Interface for creating apps called Designer.
